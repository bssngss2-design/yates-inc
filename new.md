Luck = quality of outcomes. Higher luck means: mystery boxes fail less often, roulette has better odds of landing on good segments, wandering trader offers rarer items, golden cookie gives better rewards. It makes EXISTING random events go in your favor.

Drop Chance = frequency of drops. Higher drop chance means: lottery tickets drop more often from rocks, shipment deliveries have higher chance of exotic rocks/trinkets vs money, trinket shop restocks have more rare/epic/legendary slots. It makes drops HAPPEN more, not make them better.

Luck = better quality. Drops = more quantity.
